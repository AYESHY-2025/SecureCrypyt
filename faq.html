Common Questions

What types of files can I encrypt and decrypt?

You can encrypt and decrypt text, images, files (e.g., PDFs, Word documents), audio, and video files using our platform. Our tools are designed to handle a wide range of file types securely.

Is my data safe with Secure Encrypt?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption algorithms to ensure your data is secure. Your files are processed locally on your device, and we do not store or access your data.

Do I need to create an account to use your services?

No, you do not need to create an account. Our encryption and decryption tools are free to use and do not require any registration.

How do I decrypt a file?

To decrypt a file, simply upload the encrypted file to our platform and use the same key or password you used for encryption. Our tool will decrypt the file for you.

Can I use Secure Encrypt on my mobile device?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.

What happens if I lose my encryption key?

Unfortunately, if you lose your encryption key, we cannot recover your data. We recommend storing your key in a safe place to avoid losing access to your encrypted files.
